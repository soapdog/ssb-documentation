channel message

This message is used to subscribe and unsubscribe from channels. Be aware that subscribing to a channel doesnâ€™t affect gossiping. Subscriptions are mostly hints used by applications when they need to figure out what to show to their user. A common practice for an application is to show a public feed to the user containing the messages from their followers and the messages in the channels they subscribed to.
